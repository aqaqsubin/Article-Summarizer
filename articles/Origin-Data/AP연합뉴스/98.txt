Sri Lanka Slain Journalist
FILE - In this Tuesday, Jan. 8, 2013, file photo, family members and friends light candles next to a portrait of slain newspaper editor Lasantha Wickrematunge, on his fourth death anniversary in Colombo, Sri Lanka. The daughter of the slain journalist filed a complaint Friday, Jan. 8, 2021, with the United Nations Human Rights Committee over alleged government involvement in her father’s death 12 years ago. The San Francisco-based Center for Justice and Accountability filed the complaint on behalf of Ahimsa Wickrematunge, the daughter of Lasantha, who was allegedly killed by a military-linked hit squad while driving to work on Jan. 8, 2009.
