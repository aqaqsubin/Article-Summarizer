Virus Outbreak Ukraine
Patients with COVID-19 are treated in a hospital organized in the Lviv National Medical University in Lviv, Western Ukraine, Monday, Jan. 4, 2021. The country of 42 million is recording about 9,000 new COVID-19 infections a day; more than 19,500 people have died. Ukraine imposed a wide-ranging lockdown beginning Friday Jan. 8, closing schools and entertainment venues and restaurant table service through Jan. 25. (AP Photo/Evgeniy Maloletka)
