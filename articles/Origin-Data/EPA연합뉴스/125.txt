INDONESIA PANDEMIC CORONAVIRUS COVID-19
Authorities to reimpose large-scale coronavirus restrictionsA street vendor rides past a mockup coffin of a COVID-19 victim installed on a sidewalk as part of the health protocol campaign against the coronavirus pandemic in Jakarta, Indonesia, 11 September 2020. Jakarta's authorities have decided to reimpose the large-scale coronavirus restrictions, locally known as 'PSBB' amid a rise in COVID-19 daily infections. The capital city will return to local lockdown on 14 September.  EPA/MAST IRHAM
