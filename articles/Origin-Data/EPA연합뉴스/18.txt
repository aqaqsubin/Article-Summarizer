UKRAINE IRAN AIRPLANE CRASH ANNIVERSARY
The first anniversary of the Ukraine International Airlines plane crash near TehranRelatives, colleagues, and friends of crew members of the Ukraine International Airlines Flight PS752 which crashed near Tehran a year ago attend a commemorative ceremony in Kiev, Ukraine, 08 January 2021. A Ukrainian International Airlines passenger plane was downed by Iranian armed forces on 08 January 2020 near Tehran, killing all 176 people aboard, after allegedly mistaking it for an incoming missile.
