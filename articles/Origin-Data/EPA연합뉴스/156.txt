SPAIN CATALONIA DIADA DAY PROTEST
Day of Catalonia celebrationsA group of supporters of Arran, the United People's Candidacy (CUP) youth organization, set fire to a portrait of Spain's Royal Family during the 'Day of Catalonia', or 'Diada', in Barcelona, Catalonia, north-eastern Spain, 11 September 2020. The CUP is a left-wing to far-left pro-Catalan independence and pro-Republic political party.  EPA/Quique Garcia
