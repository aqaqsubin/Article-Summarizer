NETHERLANDS GOVERNMENT CABINET
Weekly cabinet meeting in Den HagueHugo de Jonge, Dutch Minister of Health, Welfare and Sport, speaks to the press at the Binnenhof in The Hague, The Netherlands, prior to the weekly cabinet meeting, 08 January 2021. The ministers discussed, among other things, the damning report of the parliamentary committee that investigated the supplement affair, a scandal in which thousands of parents were wrongly labeled fraudsters by government officials assessing claims for child benefit payments.
