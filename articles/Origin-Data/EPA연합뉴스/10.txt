INDIA PANDEMIC CORONAVIRUS COVID19 VACCINATION TEST RUN
Covid-19 vaccination dry run in New DelhiCandidates are examined by health workers in the observation area after a mock vaccination process during a dry run at a Covid-19 vaccination centre, in New Delhi, India, 08 January 2021. According to news reports, Drug Controller General of India (DCGI) has approved the emergency use of coronavirus vaccine developed by AstraZeneca and Oxford University. Indian health authorities conducted a dry run of Covid-19 vaccination across India. India is world's second-hardest hit nation by the coronavirus disease pandemic.  EPA/RAJAT GUPTA
