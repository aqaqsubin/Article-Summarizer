PAKISTAN PROTEST
Protest against an alleged gang rape of a woman in LahoreSupporters of Islamic political party Jamaat-e-Islami hold banners reading in Urdu 'Hang publicly those who commit rape of women and children' as they shout slogans during a protest against an alleged gang rape of a woman in Lahore, Pakistan, 11 September 2020. According to reports, two persons allegedly raped the woman at a gunpoint in Gujjarpura area on 09 September, while she was waiting for help on the motorway after her car developed a fault.
