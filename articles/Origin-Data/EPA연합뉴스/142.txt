19th Anniversary of the World Trade Center attackA man places his hand on the stone at the 'Garden of Remembrance' on the 19th anniversary of the attacks on the New York City World Trade Center Towers, in Boston, Massachusetts, USA, 11 September 2020. Names of Massachusetts family members lost in the attack are inscribed in the stone monument that is located in the Boston Public Garden.  EPA/CJ GUNTHER
