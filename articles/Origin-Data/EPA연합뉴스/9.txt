Covid-19 vaccination dry run in DhramsalaA health worker takes a record of vaccine receivers during a dry run of Covid-19 vaccinations inside a Covid-19 vaccination centre at Delek Hospital in Dharamsala, India, 08 January 2021. According to news reports, Drug Controller General of India (DCGI) has approved the emergency use of coronavirus vaccine developed by AstraZeneca and Oxford University. Indian health authorities conducted a dry run of Covid-19 vaccinations across India. India is world's second-hardest hit nation by the coronavirus disease pandemic  EPA/SANJAY BAID
