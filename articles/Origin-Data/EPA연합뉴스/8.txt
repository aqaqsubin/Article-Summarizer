USA CAPITOL RIOT AFTERMATH
Aftermath of Trump supporters rioting the US CapitolMelody Black of Minnesota weeps at a makeshift monument at the West Front of the US Capitol, for Ashli Babbitt, who was shot and died the day before during a mob riot of Trump supporters at the US Capitol in Washington, DC, USA, 07 January 2021 (issued 08 January 2021). A mob of Trump supporters rioted at the US Capitol, storming inside the building, as Congress began counting the electoral college votes. Despite efforts by the mob to stop it, Congress certified Joe Biden's presidential election victory over US President Donald J. Trump.  EPA/MICHAEL REYNOLDS
