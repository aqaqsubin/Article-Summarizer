SPAIN DAY OF CATALONIA PROTEST
Diada Day of Catalonia protestA group of supporters of Arran, CUP's youth organization, set fire to a portrait of Spain's Royal Family during Day of Catalonia, or 'Diada', in Barcelona downtown, Catalonia, Spain, 11 September 2020. The United People's Candidacy (CUP) is a left-wing to far-left pro-Catalan independence and pro-Republic political party.  EPA/QUIQUE GARCIA
