CHINA BAIDU SELF DRIVING TAXI
Baidu rolls out self-driving taxi service in BeijingA test car of Baidu self-driving taxi drives on a road in Beijing, China, 11 September 2020. Chinese Internet giant Baidu rolls out its self-driving taxi service named 'Apollo Go' in Beijing. About 40 autonomous cabs are put into a trial operation initially in a trial area with a total 700 km, covering Beijing's major residential and business districts in the Yizhuang, Haidian and Shunyi districts.
