SPAIN WEATHER
Red alert in Madrid due to heavy snowfallsChildren enjoy as their father pulls their sledge across an unusual snow-covered landscape past the statue of General Martinez Campos in El Retiro park in Madrid, Spain, 08 January 2021. A total of eight parks will be closed to public in the Spanish capital as a red warning has been issued in Madrid because of heavy snowfalls expected due to storm Filomena that has already caused a big drop in temperatures and snowfalls throughout Spain except for the Canary Islands.
