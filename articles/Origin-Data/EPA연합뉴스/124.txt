POLAND MARTIAN ROBOT COMPETITION
International Martian Robot Competition in KielceA competitor adjusts a robot vehicle for the 5th edition of the International Martian Robot Competition in Kielce, south-central Poland, 11 September 2020. The European Rover Challenge (ERC) is a competition of Martian robots built by student teams and will run until 15 September. The ERC is the European version of the 'University Rover Challenge' held in the United States.  EPA/PIOTR POLAK POLAND OUT
