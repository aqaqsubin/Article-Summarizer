Covid-19 contact-tracing app will be launched across England and WalesA track and trace Q code is displayed in a cafe in London, Britain, 11 September 2020. The government has announced that a new Covid-19 contact-tracing app will be launched across England and Wales on 24 September. The app will let people scan barcode-like QR codes to register visits to hospitality venues.  EPA/NEIL HALL
