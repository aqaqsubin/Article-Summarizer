Protest against the alleged rape of a woman in PeshwarSupporters of Islamic political party Jamaat-e-Islami shout slogans and hold placards reading in Urdu 'Spread the decency Culture' during a protest against an alleged gang rape of a woman, Peshwar, Pakistan, 11 September 2020./n According to reports, two persons allegedly raped a woman at gunpoint in Gujjarpura area on 09 September, while she was waiting for help on the motorway after her car developed a fault.
