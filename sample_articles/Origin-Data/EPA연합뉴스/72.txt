Taiwan president visits missile base amid tension with ChinaA handout photo made available by Taiwan's Presidential Office shows President Tsai Ing-wen (Front-L) visiting a missile base in northern Taiwan, 11 September 2020, as China intensifies military threat against Taiwan. Tsai thanked the soldiers for guarding Taiwan's sky and keeping Taiwan safe. She urged them 'not give an inch' in defending Taiwan's sovereignty, democracy and freedom.
