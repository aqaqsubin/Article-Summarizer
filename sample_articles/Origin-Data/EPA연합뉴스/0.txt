SPAIN CORONAVIRUS TRACKERS
Spanish army to help in the traces against Covid-19A member of the UME (Spain's Army Emergency Unit) works as a rastreator of contacts of Covid-19 patients at Torrejon de Ardoz air base in Madrid province, central Spain, 11 September 2020, as part of the 'Baluarte Operation'. By the named operation, UME's military men and women help to fight against Coronavirus by tracing contacts of people with a possitive RPC on Covid-19, so as the virus stop spreading.
