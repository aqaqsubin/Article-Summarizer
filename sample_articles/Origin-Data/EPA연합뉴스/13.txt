Fire at port of Beirutepaselect epa08660806 Firefighters extinguish a fire in a warehouse at the port of Beirut, Lebanon, 11 September 2020. A fire broke out on 10 September in an oils and tires warehouse in the port's duty-free zone, just weeks after the 04 August Beirut port blast in which at least 190 people died, more than 6,000 were injured, and the port area and large parts of the city were devastated. According to reports, the fire is brought under control.  EPA/WAEL HAMZEH
