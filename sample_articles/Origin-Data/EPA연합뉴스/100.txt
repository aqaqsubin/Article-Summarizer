JAPAN BRITAIN TRADE DEAL
Japan and Britain reached a free trade agreementJapanese Foreign Minister Toshimitsu Motegi (L) speaks with Britain's Secretary of State of International Trade Liz Truss during a video conference in Tokyo, Japan, 11 September 2020. Trade negotiations between Japan and Britain reached an agreement as Foreign Minister Motegi and UK Secretary of State of International Trade Truss had a final discussion during a video conference and agreed on tariffs for Japanese cars and British agricultural products.
